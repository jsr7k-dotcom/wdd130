Featured Adventures

The Serpent's Tail (Full Day)

For the ultimate thrill-seekers! Tackle Class IV rapids on this action-packed journey through the heart of the canyon. Not for the faint of heart, this trip is a true test of courage and teamwork.

Canyon Float (Half Day)

Perfect for families and first-timers. Enjoy the stunning scenery and gentle waters of the upper river. This relaxing trip offers plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife spotting.

Sunset Run (2-Hour)

Experience the magic of the canyon as the sun goes down. This beautiful short trip is perfect for all skill levels and offers some of the most spectacular photo opportunities on the river.
